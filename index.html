About Me

I've been writing code that computers love to read since the late '90s—and now I'm exploring how AI can help write code that humans love to read too.

Originally from Virginia, I made the cross-country move to the Bay Area and haven't looked back. I hold dual bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics from Francis Marion University—a combination that fuels my love for solving hard problems.

Currently, I'm a Senior Software Architect at Synergi Partners, building software that enhances business processes and improves operational performance.

When I step away from the keyboard, you'll find me spending time with family or snowboarding.
